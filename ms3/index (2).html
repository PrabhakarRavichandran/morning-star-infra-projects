What is the home construction cost per sq ft in Chennai 2026?+

Home construction costs in Chennai in 2026 typically range from ₹1,800 to ₹3,200 per sq. ft. depending on specifications. Morning Star Infra offers 4 packages: Basic (₹1,980), Classic (₹2,430), Premium (₹2,860), and Green Eco (₹2,990) — all fully transparent with no hidden charges.

Do you handle CMDA and DTCP approvals?+

Yes. We provide complete documentation support for CMDA/DTCP approvals. The approval fees are borne by the client, but our team handles all technical drawings, coordination, and submission processes.

What is a Defect Liability Bond and do you provide it?+

A Defect Liability Bond is a legally binding document that commits the contractor to rectify any structural defects within the specified period at no cost to the owner. Morning Star provides a 5-year bond aligned with RERA structural defect standards — issued at handover.

Can Morning Star assist with home loan processing?+

Absolutely. We provide all technical documents required by banks including structural drawings, BOQ, cost estimates, and stage completion certificates needed for home loan disbursement at each construction milestone.

How long does it take to build a standard 2000 sq. ft. house?+

A typical 2000 sq. ft. residential build takes 10–14 months depending on design complexity, number of floors, and approval timelines. We provide a detailed project schedule with milestones before construction begins.

REPAIR CAPABILITIES

Our Repair & Retrofitting Arsenal

Micro-Concreting
Precision injection and micro-concrete application to restore degraded concrete sections. Restores full structural capacity of damaged columns, beams, and slabs without demolition.
Carbon FRP Wrapping
Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer wrapping of columns and beams for seismic retrofitting and load capacity enhancement. Dramatically extends structural life without adding dead load.
Structural Jacketing
RCC, steel, or GFRP jacketing of columns and beams to increase cross-section and load capacity. Ideal for buildings undergoing additional floor construction or usage change.
Crack Injection & Repair
Epoxy and polyurethane resin injection for structural and non-structural cracks. Halts moisture ingress, prevents rebar corrosion, and seals the concrete matrix permanently.
Anti-Carbonation Coating
Specialized coatings to halt the carbonation process in aging concrete, protecting rebar from corrosion and extending the building's service life by 20–30 years.
Structural Engineering Design
Forensic structural analysis, load assessment, and retrofitting design by our post-graduate engineers. Includes issuance of Form B Structural Stability Certificate under TN law.
OUR PROCESS

From Diagnosis to Certification

1. Visual Inspection & Distress Survey
Our engineers conduct a comprehensive visual survey, mapping spalling, cracking, efflorescence, and rebar exposure across the structure.
2. NDT Testing & Concrete Core Analysis
UPV, rebound hammer, and core testing are conducted using our owned equipment per IS 13311 and IS 516 standards.
3. Structural Assessment Report
Detailed engineering report outlining concrete strength, rebar condition, and structural adequacy — with photographic evidence.
4. Repair Specification & BOQ
A complete repair methodology, material specifications, and transparent bill of quantities — before a single rupee is committed.
5. Supervised Repair Execution
All repair work executed by trained applicators under the direct supervision of our structural engineers.
6. Form B Certification
Post-repair, we issue the Structural Stability Certificate (Form B) under the Tamil Nadu Public Buildings Act — legally certifying the building's restored integrity.

BIS CODE COMPLIANCE

Bureau of Indian Standards

IS 13311 Part 1 — Ultrasonic Pulse Velocity
Method of non-destructive testing of concrete. Morning Star's NDT equipment is calibrated and operated strictly per IS 13311 Part 1 for pulse velocity measurement to assess concrete quality and uniformity.
IS 13311 Part 2 — Rebound Hammer
Assessment of concrete surface hardness and estimation of compressive strength per IS 13311 Part 2. Used alongside UPV for comprehensive concrete condition profiling.
IS 516 — Concrete Core Testing
Standard methods of test for strength of concrete. Core samples extracted from existing structures are tested per IS 516 to establish actual in-situ concrete strength.
IS 456 — Plain & Reinforced Concrete
All new construction and repair work conforms to IS 456:2000 — the primary Indian code for plain and reinforced concrete design, including mix design, durability, and cover specifications.
IS 800 — Steel Structures
All PEB and structural steel designs follow IS 800:2007 — the general construction in steel code — for member design, connections, and fabrication standards.
IS 1893 — Seismic Design
All structural designs consider seismic zone requirements per IS 1893 — essential for Chennai's Zone II/III classification — ensuring earthquake-resistant construction.

FAQ
How often should I perform NDT on aging buildings?+

For buildings older than 15 years, schedule NDT every 3–5 years or after major events (floods, heavy rains, or structural alterations). We use UPV, rebound hammer and core testing to form a reliable diagnostic baseline.

What is Form B and do you issue it?+

Form B is the Structural Stability Certificate required under the Tamil Nadu Public Buildings Act. Morning Star is authorised to perform the inspection and issue Form B after a certified assessment.

Do you assist with CMDA / DTCP approvals and permits?+

Yes. We prepare technical drawings, structural calculations and liaise with authorities to support CMDA/DTCP approvals. Approval fees are paid by the client, while we handle submissions and revisions.

Which cities and regions do you serve?+

We are Chennai-based and serve projects across Tamil Nadu including SIPCOT/industrial corridors. For larger out-of-state projects we provide feasibility consulting and partner execution support.

What warranty do you provide on constructions and fitouts?+

We provide a 5-year Defect Liability Bond for structural works (RERA-aligned) and manufacturer-backed warranties for interior fittings per the package selected.

How do your home construction packages differ?+

Our packages vary by materials, finishes and inclusions—from Basic (value-focused) to Green Eco (solar-ready, IGBC consultation). Every package details materials, BOQ and inclusions to eliminate hidden costs.

Can you perform full turnkey interior fitouts for existing homes?+

Yes. We handle site measurement, 3D design, in-house manufacturing and full installation for resale apartments, new homes, and commercial premises.

What is PEB and do you build PEB structures?+

PEB stands for Pre-Engineered Building. We deliver PEB structures for warehouses and factories with engineered steel frames, fast assembly and compliance for industrial clients.

How do you price structural repair and retrofitting?+

After a diagnostic survey and NDT, we produce a repair methodology and transparent BOQ. Pricing is itemised (materials, labour, supervision) and presented before work begins.

Do you subcontract specialist trades?+

We maintain preferred subcontractors for electrical, HVAC and specialised services, but core structural and interior works are executed or directly supervised by our teams to ensure quality control.

What materials and brands do you use?+

We specify industry-leading materials: ARS / Tata TMT, Ramco / Dalmia cement, premium sanitary brands and certified electrical fittings. All brands are listed in project BOQs for client review.

How do change requests during construction get handled?+

Change requests are managed via documented variation orders with revised BOQ and timelines. We require sign-off and any incremental payment terms before proceeding.

What is your project supervision model?+

Every project is assigned a Project Manager and a registered structural engineer who perform regular site audits, quality checks and milestone sign-offs to ensure engineering compliance and schedule adherence.

How do I request a quote or site assessment?+

Use the "Get a Free Quote" button or the WhatsApp quick-link. Provide site location, approximate built-up area and preferred timeline; we respond with a site-assessment schedule within 24 hours.

Do you provide sustainability / green building options?+

Yes. Our Green Eco package includes solar-ready roofs, rainwater harvesting and energy-efficient glazing. We also advise on IGBC-friendly specifications for clients seeking certification.

How is safety managed on site?+

Safety is enforced via toolbox talks, PPE requirements, periodic inspections and documented method statements for high-risk activities. We maintain insurance and incident reporting as part of our site governance.
